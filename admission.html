2. Qualification for Admission Test:

2.1. For Group A: Any candidate from Science of Intermediate and Secondary Education Boards/Madrasah Board who completed his/her S.S.C or equivalent in 2011 or 2012 and H.S.C or equivalent in 2013 or 2014 can apply for the admission test. To apply student must have at least GPA 3.5 in S.S.C or equivalent and at least GPA 3.0 including 4th subject in H.S.C or equivalent, must have at least a total GPA of 6.5. But if any candidate have below grade C in any subject in H.S.C, his/her application will not be accepted.

Students of GCE 'O' level examination must pass 5 subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology and must pass 2 subjects out of Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics. But if any candidate have below grade C in any subject in ‘O’ level and ‘A’ level, his/her application will not be accepted.

For Group B: Any candidate from Science of Intermediate and Secondary Education Boards/Madrasah Board who completed his/her S.S.C or equivalent in 2011 or 2012 and H.S.C or equivalent in 2013 or 2014 can apply for the admission test. To apply student must have at least GPA 3.5 in S.S.C or equivalent and at least GPA 3.0 including 4th subject in H.S.C or equivalent, must have at least a total GPA of 6.5. But if any candidate have below grade C in any subject in H.S.C, his/her application will not be accepted.

Candidate from GCE 'O' level examination must pass 5 subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology and must pass 2 subjects out of Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics. But if any candidate have below grade C in any subject in ‘O’ level and ‘A’ level, his/her application will not be accepted.

For Group C: For ‘English’ any candidate from Science/Business/Humanities of Intermediate and Secondary Education Boards/Madrasah Board who completed his/her S.S.C or equivalent in 2011 or 2012 and H.S.C or equivalent in 2013 or 2014 can apply for the admission test. To apply student of Humanities must have at least GPA 3.0 in S.S.C or equivalent and at least GPA 3.0 including 4th Subject in H.S.C or equivalent, must have at least a total GPA of 6.0. But if any candidate have below grade C in any subject in H.S.C, his/her application will not be accepted. Students of Science and Business must have at least GPA 3.5 in S.S.C or equivalent and at least GPA 3.0 including 4th Subject in H.S.C or equivalent, must have at least a total GPA of 6.5.

Candidate must have Grade B in English from GCE 'O' level and ‘A’ level. Below grade C in any subject in ‘O’ level and ‘A’ level will be rejected.

For Group D: For ‘Business Administration’ and ‘Economics and Poverty Studies’, any candidate from Science/Business/Humanities of Intermediate and Secondary Education Boards/Madrasah Board who completed his/her S.S.C or equivalent in 2011 or 2012 and H.S.C or equivalent in 2013 or 2014 can apply for the admission test. To apply student of Humanities must have at least GPA 3.0 in S.S.C or equivalent and at least GPA 3.0 including 4th Subject in H.S.C or equivalent, must have at least a total GPA of 6.0. But if any candidate have below grade C in any subject in H.S.C, his/her application will not be accepted. Students of Science and Business must have at least GPA 3.5 in S.S.C or equivalent and at least GPA 3.0 including 4th Subject in H.S.C or equivalent, must have at least a total GPA of 6.5.

Candidate must have Grade B in English from GCE 'O' level and ‘A’ level. Below grade C in any subject in ‘O’ level and ‘A’ level will be rejected.

2.2. Students who completed their S.S.C or equivalent of GCE 'O' level before 2011, cannot apply for the admission test.

2.3. To participate in admission test student must have Mathematics in H.S.C for group A and group B.

2.4. Foreign student can apply directly, but will be qualified for admission after approval of the certificates attached with the application by the Equivalence Committee of the university.

3. Rules for apply (application via mobile phone):
To apply for the admission test candidate should send an SMS to 16222 from a Teletalk prepaid mobile phone.

3.1. To apply from a prepaid Teletalk mobile phone, go to message option, write NSTU, follow by a space, then write first three letter of the HSC education board, follow by a space, then write HSC roll number, follow by a space, then write pass year of HSC, follow by a space, then write first three letter of SSC education board, follow by a space, write SSC roll number, follow by a space, then write pass year of SSC, follow by a space, write the keyword of the group and send it to 16222.

Example:
NSTU<space>COM<space>123456<space>2014<space>COM<space>123456<space>2012<space>A

The example is for comilla board. Candidate must write his/her HSC and SSC roll number in the place of 123456. If any candidate pass HSC in 2013, he must write 2013 instead of 2014. Similarly, to apply in group B candidate must write B instead of A. For group C candidate must write C instead of A, and for D, candidate must write D instead of A. For other boards instead of comilla write SYL(Sylhet), BAR(Barisal), CHI(Chittagong), DHA(Dhaka), DIN(Dinajpur), JES(Jessor),RAJ(Rajshahi), MAD(Madrasah).

3.2. After the above message is sent, a message will be back for confirmation. This message will contain the name of the candidate, group name, and fee for the form and a PIN number. After the message is received, candidate must sent a confirmation message. To send confirmation message, go to message option, write NSTU, follow by a space, then write YES, follow by a space, then write the given PIN number in the previous message, follow by a space, write a mobile number for communication and send it t0 16222.

Example: NSTU<space>YES<space>654321<space>01XXXXXXXXX

In the place of 654321, candidate must write the PIN number given to him/her. It is notable that only after the confirmation message the fees for admission will be deducted, otherwise no fee will be deducted.

4. Especially Notable:
    A teletalk number can be used for more than one application but applicant must provide another mobile number for further contact.

    Application process can be performed anytime even in holidays.

    Individual application SMS is needed for group A, B, C and D.

    The admission fees are mentioned with vat. If any candidate apply for a group by mistaken it cannot be undone.

    Students of science background in HSC or equivalent can apply for all four groups. Others can only apply in C and D.

    Different merit list will be made for group A, B, C and D.

    In the admission test, applicant must bring two attested photocopies of HSC original registration and two passport size photo. He/she must write his/her name and roll number (in Bangla and English both) that he/she get through SMS on the other side of the photo. Each individual photo must be attached by the staple with the attested photocopy of registration card on the top left side. On the top of the registration card he/she must write the roll number in Bangla and English. Without these things no applicant can seat for exam. The authority of university will authorize one of these two registration card as admit card of the student. This copy must be kept safely by the applicant. The authority will kept the other copy of the registration card for further procedure.

    Applicant from GCE O/A level must bring the attested photocopy of the grade sheet of O and A level.

    Admission in Quota: For freedom fighter quota and tribe quota 5% of seats will be preserved.

    No Electronic devices e.g. computer/tab, mobile phone, Programmable Calculator, Programmable watch will not be allowed. But applicant can bring CASIO-FX 570 MS or below model general calculator with him/her. ES series calculator is not allowed.

    For admission test date please see the notice board in the website.

5. Admission Test (Admission test will be taken by MCQ)

6. For Group A: In Bangla & English, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology, each subject will contain 25 questions. The number of total questions are 125. Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics are compulsory for the candidate. Candidate will have to choose either Biology or Bangla and English for answering question. But for Environmental and Hazard Studies, candidate must answer Biology. Total marks are 100. Pass marks are 40.

For Group B: There will be 25 questions from each of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology. Total marks are 100. Pass marks are 40.

For Group C: The test will be taken on Bangla and English. Total marks are 100. 60 Marks for English and 40 marks for Bangla. Pass marks are 40. But candidate must get 30 marks in English and 10 marks in Bangla.

For Group D: For "Business Administration" and "Economics and Poverty Studies" admission test, the marks distribution are below:


Mathematics: 30 marks (Secondary level)
Analytical Ability: 30 marks
English: 30 marks &
General Knowledge: 10 marks.
Total marks are 100. Pass marks are 40.

6.1. MCQ answer sheet will be provided by the university. In the answer sheet the right circle must be filled up with ball point pen. The rules for answering question will be provided with the question. Each student will get only one answer sheet. Mobile phone is not allowed in the examination hall.

6.2. The question paper will be prepared based on the Intermediate level syllabus.

7. Student Fee-Charge: During admission each student may have to pay BDT 20000 taka approximately including admission charges for first term.

8. Merit Score and Merit List:

8.1. Merit list will be prepared with on 200 marks.

i) GPA of SSC/Equivalent with 4th subject will be multiplied with 8.

ii) GPA of SSC/Equivalent with 4th subject will be multiplied with 12.

iii) Total marks in admission test.

8.2. If more students get similar merit score in the admission test then the HSC and SSC grade of the candidates will get priority.

8.3. For selection according to merit, university will call the candidate who will pass in the admission test. In this case, choosing of subject will get priority. If any seat is left, university will call the candidates from waiting list.

9. In the admission day, the original grade sheet of SSC and HSC must be submitted to the convener of the admission processing committee. The grade sheets must be submitted in a time duration. If the duration over without submission the selection of the candidate will be automatically rejected. In the empty seat next candidate will be selected upon completion of the admission conditions. After subject choosing, the selected candidates must pay the admission fees and charges in the Agrani Bank Limited, NSTU branch.
